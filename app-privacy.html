2. Why we use it

  • To perform and record solar assessments and installations.
  • To verify genuine on‑site attendance and protect against poor work, fraud or tampering — evidence is geotagged, content‑hashed and locked after submission.
  • To schedule jobs, calculate fees and superannuation, and support customer protection, warranty and compliance.

We do not use your data for advertising and do not track you across other companies’ apps or sites.

3. Sharing

Job evidence is shared with the customer and the installer responsible for their job, and with service providers who host the platform (our cloud database/storage provider) under confidentiality. We do not sell personal information. We may disclose where required by law. Hosting may be outside Australia [confirm region], handled consistently with the APPs.

4. Retention

Records and evidence are kept for the life of the job and any warranty/compliance obligations, then deleted or de‑identified. Submitted evidence is immutable (locked and hash‑verified).

5. Account deletion & your rights

Each app has a “Delete my account” action (also at /apply.html). It deactivates your access immediately and requests erasure of your personal data, subject to records we must keep by law. You may also request access or correction. Contact [privacy@yourdomain.com.au].

6. Security

Data is encrypted in transit, access is restricted by role, and evidence photos are content‑hashed so tampering is detectable.
