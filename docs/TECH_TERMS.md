# Sales Technician — Terms & Conditions (contractor)

> **Draft for legal review — not legal advice.** This is the plain-English basis for the
> agreement a technician accepts at onboarding (recorded as `sales_reps.contractor_terms_at`
> + `contractor_terms_ref`). Have a lawyer finalise it and confirm the engagement genuinely
> supports contractor status (whole-of-relationship test) before use.

## 1. Engagement
You are engaged as an **independent contractor** (sole trader), not an employee. You hold
your own **ABN**, run your own business, choose which jobs to accept, and set your own
availability. Nothing here creates an employment, partnership or agency relationship.

## 2. Your obligations (kit & compliance)
As a condition of engagement you must, and must keep, current:
- A **drone** (sub-250g) plus the **CASA legals to fly it for work** (drone registration +
  RPA Operator Accreditation), a smartphone, and a tape measure.
- A valid **police check** and any licences required for the work.
- **You must never access or climb a roof.** All roof capture is ground / drone / satellite
  only. Roof condition is verified by the licensed installer, not you.

## 3. Fees & superannuation
- Completed job: **$50** (or **$45** while earning a company-provided drone, which becomes
  yours after 50 completed jobs). No-access visits are **$0**.
- **Superannuation** is paid **in addition**, as its own line (currently **12%** of the fee),
  in line with superannuation guarantee obligations.
- You invoice for the work; you are responsible for your own tax and (if applicable) GST.

## 4. Evidence & honesty (integrity)
Every job must be captured honestly and on site:
- Photos are **geotagged and time-stamped**; a no-access claim requires a confirmed booking,
  **on-site GPS**, a reason, and geotagged evidence. Submitted evidence is **locked and
  hash-verified** — it cannot be altered after submission.
- You must not falsify, stage, edit, re-use, or tamper with any photo, GPS location, call
  record, or job outcome, or claim a fee for work not genuinely performed.

## 5. Immediate suspension or ban — integrity breaches
**Dishonesty, tampering, or fraud results in immediate suspension and/or a permanent ban
from the app**, at Solarsearch's discretion, including (without limitation): faking or
editing evidence, spoofing GPS/location, claiming jobs not attended or not genuinely done,
false no-access claims, or misrepresenting the work.
- **Suspended** = access is switched off pending review (reversible).
- **Banned** = permanent removal from the app.
- Enforcement is immediate in the system (status change cuts off job access at once) and is
  **logged** (`tech_status_log`). Suspension/ban does not limit any other remedy Solarsearch
  or an affected customer may have. Fees for affected/fraudulent jobs may be withheld or
  reversed.

## 6. Customer & data
Treat customer sites, details and data as confidential; use them only to perform the job.
Comply with privacy obligations and the SMS/consent terms in the app.

## 7. Insurance & liability
You are responsible for your own conduct on site and are encouraged to hold appropriate
**public liability / professional indemnity** insurance. You are personally liable for your
own debts and acts as a sole trader.

## 8. Changes & termination
Either party may end the engagement on reasonable notice. Solarsearch may update these terms;
continued use of the app after an update is acceptance. Integrity breaches (s5) allow
immediate action without notice.
