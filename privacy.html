2. Why we use it

  • If you opt in, to send you updates about rebate changes or pricing in your area.

3. Who we share it with

We do not sell your personal information. We disclose it only:

  • To installers you choose — your details are shared with an accredited installer only when you explicitly select them (up to three, and possibly none). Until then, no installer sees your contact details.
  • To service providers that help us operate — for example our hosting and database provider (Supabase), email/SMS providers, and analytics tools — who are bound to use the information only to provide their service to us.
  • Where required by law, or to protect our rights, safety, or property.
